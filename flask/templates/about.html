About

This site has benefitted from funding from the Northern Ireland Department of Finance Open Data Innovation and Outreach Fund, to assist with its development in 2021.

Data are updated twice a week, using sources:

  • Assembly details from the official NI Assembly site; in particular, the AIMS Portal
  • Tweets from Twitter, gathered using rtweet Tweet tracking is currently inactive following Twitter API changes
  • News articles from NewsCatcher
  • Polling data from any relevant source, which for Northern Ireland is almost exclusively LucidTalk

Plots are created with Altair, a Python interface to Vega-Lite. Other displays use a few JavaScript/jQuery libraries, including DataTables, Sparklines, ProgressPieSVG, and autoComplete.

The data science parts of the site use a little , and more Python: NumPy, pandas, Gensim, NLTK, scikit-learn, and HuggingFace.

The site is built with Python and Flask and is hosted on .

Original artwork is by Ellistration.

Other sites in this niche to check out:

  • Electoral Calculus: UK polling and election predictions, with many good articles on methodology, and now with fairly good coverage of Northern Ireland too
  • They Work For You: includes full Plenary debate proceedings
  • Govtrackr: topic analysis of MPs' tweets
  • GovTrack: big site with voting record stats for the US system; includes a good explanation of the principal components 'member average position' method
  • Slugger O'Toole: the leading source of commentary, often data-driven, on NI politics
  • And Vote View, Open Secrets, Vote Smart, Clear The Lobby, Democracy Club, Flavible Politics (no longer updating), ElectionMapsUK, The Public Whip
