What They Say

Some of the NI MLAs and MPs are active on Bluesky, so tracking this activity can provide some insight into their political outlook, and their engagement with the public and their colleagues. Posts are collected weekly, starting from 2023.

Twitter is the more popular platform, but it no longer allows programmatic access to tweet data at an affordable price, so was discontinued on NI PoliGraph in mid-2023.

Technical Stuff

  • Bluesky data
  • Post sentiment

Posts are collected from Bluesky once a week. The number of reposts for each pos is recorded at this time, so some posts will have had a full week to collect reposts, while others will have had only a day or less. The repost rates should therefore be taken as the average number of reposts obtained in the first 3-4 days of a post's lifetime.

Blueksy post sentiment is calculated using the VADER algorithm, which is specifically geared towards social media content. These raw values are usually positive, but the overall mean is removed, to leave the scores for the most positive and negative posters as positive and negative deviations of comparable magnitude.
